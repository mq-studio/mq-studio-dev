## How to Use Templates

1. **Copy** the appropriate template
2. **Rename** it to match your content (use lowercase and hyphens)
3. **Move** it to the correct content folder
4. **Fill in** your information
5. **Save** and you're done!

## Tips

- Always keep the `---` lines at the top and bottom
- Fill in required fields (marked with `*REQUIRED`)
- Delete optional fields you don't need
- Use YYYY-MM-DD format for dates
- Look at existing content files for examples

## Need Help?

The templates have inline comments explaining each field. If you're unsure, just follow the template structure and read the comments!
